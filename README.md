## Creative Coding – Quiz 8
*Reinterpreting Marilyn Monroe (1967) by Andy Warhol* 

<br>

## Part 1 – Inspiration 
<br>
I want to recreate the bold colours, repetition, and imperfect textures of Andy Warhol’s Marilyn Monroe. The bright Pop Art style feels energetic, playful, and iconic. Using p5.js can transform the static artwork into an interactive digital experience with movement, colour shifts, and layered effects, while still keeping Warhol’s expressive aesthetic.

## Part 2 — Coding Technique Exploration
<br>

***Technique: Perlin Noise + Randomness for Colour Variation*** 

<br>
Perlin noise and randomness can recreate the imperfect printing style of Pop Art. Random colours make every portrait unique, while Perlin noise creates smooth movement and textured distortions. Together, these techniques can turn the artwork into a vibrant, evolving digital composition that feels expressive, dynamic, and less mechanical.
<br>
